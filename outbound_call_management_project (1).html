Outbound Call Management Module in Salesforce

I designed a Salesforce module that helps teams manage outbound calls in a simple, structured, and traceable way. The solution made it easier for operators to log, classify, and close calls while keeping data complete, consistent, and connected across the business.

Project Overview

The module was created to support outbound call operations in Salesforce with a cleaner process and stronger controls. It gave operators a clear path to follow, reduced manual input, and ensured every call was properly linked to the right business record.

Problem

Outbound call activity needed a more structured process so teams could capture the right information, follow consistent business rules, and avoid losing context across cases, contacts, tasks, and work orders.

Solution

I designed a call management module in Salesforce with clear classification logic, automatic field completion, validation rules, permissions, and built-in tracking of unsuccessful calls.

Process & Functional Design

My role focused on translating business needs into a process that was easy to use, technically consistent, and strong enough to support governance and reporting.

Process & Logic Design

  • Mapped how outbound calls can start from different Salesforce objects such as cases, tasks, contacts, and work orders.
  • Defined rules to auto-fill key fields and reduce manual work for operators.
  • Ensured every call was connected to the correct record so the full business context stayed visible.

Functional Design

  • Created a clear classification structure using Type → Motive → Cause.
  • Defined mandatory completion rules before a call could be closed.
  • Set permission-based editing for important fields and sensitive actions.
  • Added logic to detect unsuccessful calls such as voicemails or calls that were too short.

UX/UI & Data Quality

  • Designed a simple user flow so operators always knew what step came next.
  • Added warnings when required information was missing.
  • Made changes traceable for transparency, auditing, and accountability.

Key Features

The final design combined usability, automation, and control to support day-to-day call operations without sacrificing data quality.

Click-to-call from multiple Salesforce objects

Users could launch outbound calls directly from the records they were already working in.

Automatic field completion

Relevant information was pre-filled to reduce repetitive work and improve consistency.

Clear classification system

The Type → Motive → Cause structure helped standardize how call outcomes were captured.

Permission-based editing and validation

Rules controlled who could edit certain fields and prevented incomplete call closures.

Links between calls, cases, and tasks

Each call remained tied to the right operational context for better tracking and reporting.

Impact

This module improved both the operator experience and the quality of the data generated by the process, creating a more reliable foundation for business visibility and compliance.

Faster Handling
